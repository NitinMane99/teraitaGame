Instructions

Welcome to Teraita Games World! Here are the instructions for each game:

Number Guessing Game:

  • Enter the maximum number to set the range for the random number.
  • Guess a number within that range.
  • Receive feedback on whether your guess is too high or too low.
  • Try to guess the number correctly to win!

Snake Game:

  • Use the arrow keys to control the snake's movement.
  • Try to eat the red food to grow the snake and increase your score.
  • Avoid colliding with the walls or the snake's own body.
  • Score as high as possible before the game ends.

Rock-Paper-Scissors:

  • Click on Rock, Paper, or Scissors to make your choice.
  • The computer will make its choice.
  • Rock beats Scissors, Scissors beats Paper, and Paper beats Rock.
  • Try to win against the computer!
